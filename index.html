Inspiration
The Legibility of Serif and Sans Serif Typefaces
Different Fonts Increase Reading Speed for Different Individuals

My mom reading on iPad Minis for the last 15 years — because the regular iPad is too big for train commutes, and a phone is too small. And seeing her read introduced me to the fact that reading apps let users change the font.
